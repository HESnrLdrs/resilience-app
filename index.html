The Resilience Imperative

Welcome to this essential exploration of resilience - not as a buzzword, but as a strategic leadership capability. After 30+ years working with senior academic leaders across nearly 50 UK universities, I've witnessed the unique pressures facing higher education today.

From REF deadlines and budget constraints to media scrutiny and organisational change, academic leadership demands more than intellectual excellence. It requires the ability to remain effective, innovative, and human whilst navigating unprecedented challenges.

What you'll achieve:

  • Assess your current resilience foundation
  • Build practical strategies for challenging periods
  • Deploy resilience skills in real-time
  • Create sustainable practices for long-term maintenance
